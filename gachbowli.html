A Teenage girl hangs herself-4th October

Two days after her boyfriend died, a teenage girl hanged herself at Gachibowli on Tuesday. Police said Neha,19, was in love with one Salman. According to the police, the woman worked in a food court located at Nanakramguda where her male friend was also employed. Both of them were in a relationship and had intended to marry but their parents did not agree over the proposal. On Sunday, Salman hanged himself at his room in Venkatapuram at Ranga Reddy district. After coming to know about his death, Neha on Tuesday hanged herself to death. It was at 10 am on Tuesday that she was found hanging by her family members at their residence. Police registered a case under section 174 (suspicious death) Cr.Pc..

Flyover took yong technian life - 25 th July

A young technician of Telangana Institute of Medical Sciences (TIMS) fell off the Gachibowli Biodiversity flyover on Sunday evening and died when the bike he was riding crashed into the flyover railing. The accident took place at 4 pm when the bike driven by B Madhu, 24, fell off the level 2 flyover on to the level 1. Madhu died on the spot while his friend Machagiri, 24, who was pillion-riding, suffered serious injuries. Police suspect the accident took place due to speeding. Madhu was suspected to be in an inebriated condition when he crashed the bike into the railing, police said. Madhu along with Giri was heading to IKEA from Raidurgam. Such was the impact of the crash that after hitting the railing of the flyover, the bike fell on the second flyover beneath it. A native of Siddipet, Madhu was residing in a PG hostel at Gachibowli. The condition of Machagiri, who hails from Bhuvanagiri district, is critical. A case was registered under section 304-A (negligence causing death) of IPC. This is second accident where a vehicle flew off the Biodiversity flyover and crashed below leading to the death of a person. On November 23, 2019, a woman was killed in a freak accident when a car fell on a 56-year-old woman below the flyover after the driver failed to negotiate a sharp curve due to speeding.
