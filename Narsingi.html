Intermediate Student dies by suicide

Upset with his performance in Intermediate exams, a 16-year-old student, Sai Teja, died by suicide at his Narsingi home on Friday. Son of a pot maker, he was enrolled with a private college in Langar Houz. Sai was the youngest of three siblings. At 9am on Friday, Sai along with his father, Ramulu, went to Manchirevula circle to sell pots. An hour later, he returned home to fetch more pots. When he didn't turn up at the market, his sister, Anusha, went to check. "The main door was ajar and when Anusha went inside, she found Sai hanging by a nylon rope," Narsingi police said. Sai's parents rushed back took him to a private hospital in Narsingi. He was then shifted to Golconda Area Hospital, where doctors declared him brought dead. A suspicious death case was registered under Section 174 of CrPC.
